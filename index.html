Range of Optimus engineering

We have experience in small and medium-sized buildings, bridge design, maritime structures, prestressed structures, geotehnical design, nonlinear analyses and seismic design of structures.
Particular attention during the design we give to the feasibility of our facilities, as well as the quality and quantity of materials and construction cost price.

Structural engineering

Our design is mostly based on Eurocodes. For analyses we use various programs in structural engineering, depending on the type of facility, to have the best correspondence with reality.


Earthquake engineering

The seismic stability of the new facilities we ensure through Eurocode 8. Fragility and seismic resistance of existing facilities we can estimate on the basis of nonlinear analysis and real hapend earthquakes.

Details of structures

We make projects to the smallest details, easily legible, very comprehensive, so that the construction of buildings with our drawings becomes easy way.

Geotehnical and hydrotechnical engineering

In addition to the stability and bearing capacity, we pay great attention to the integration of objects into a spatial enviroment.

Assessment and retrofitting

We can give new strength to the old facilities through various methods of rehabilitation.

Materials

The choice of materials and its quality are essential for the lifetime of the building and your pleasant stay in it. For objects in which people live, we recommend the greatest use of natural materials such as stone and wood in combination with concrete.

Science and research

We are open-minded for research and experiments in field of new materials and techniques in construction.
Feel free to contact us.
